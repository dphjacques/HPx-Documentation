stagnantgasphase

-stagnantphase <bool:stagnantphase>



Indicate if transport in the gas phase is only by diffusion (true) or also advective (false). As HYDRUS does not include the coupled water and air movement, advective transport is approximated using the assumptions described in section 5.1 of the HYDRUS-1D manual [Simunek et al., 2018].



stagnantphase

bool: if true, gas phase is stagnant and only diffusive transport is simulated. If false, advective gas phase transport is approximated and advective-diffusive transport is simulated. Default: true.
